Guanya Shi 石冠亚

I am an Assistant Professor in the Robotics Institute and the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU). I lead the LeCAR (Learning and Control for Agile Robotics) Lab. I also have courtesy faculty positions in the Mechanical Engineering and Electrical and Computer Engineering Departments.

I completed my Ph.D. in 2022 from Caltech, advised by Soon-Jo Chung and Yisong Yue. I received a B.E. from Tsinghua University in 2017. From 2022 to 2023, I was a postdoctoral scholar in the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Washington with Byron Boots.
